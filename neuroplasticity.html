### Research Neuroplasticity

Discuss how understanding the principles of neuroplasticity benefits people
- Understanding the principles of neuroplasticity opens our world to the fact that when we learn, we form new pathways in the brain. We can figure out how to purposefully rewire the brain, that we can get smarter, better, or more skilled at something through sustained effort!

Discuss how you might engage with the principles of neuroplasticity for your own benefit
- I read this in an article of how to get started and I was so surprised to see that I had started going it already! Here goes:

Challenge > Intention > Specific Attention > Repetition & Intensity > Time

Here's how the example works for me:

Challenge (coding) > Intention (For me it's creating kickass design projects) > Specific Attention (Plan. Structure. Goal. Incentive) > Repetition & Intensity (Check in) > Time (Plan for lapses – it’s normal. Refocus, adjust, keep going.)

What are some of the ways to increase your neuroplasticity?
- Mindfulness.
- Journaling and meditating. Even if it's just 5 mins a day. I also enjoy a quick sketch or painting to help set the day right.
- Some form of exercise. Or dance.
- Sleep and eat well.

### Research Growth Mindset

Discuss what it is and why it is relevant
- Neuroplasticity and a growth mindset mirror each other. It can help you overcome obstacles when learning something new. It helps you understand the importance of persistence and determination, and the fact that you can control/rewire your brain to be that way. Game change, I'm tell you! By changing the way you think, you can change the way you learn and that's essential when immersing yourself in a 15 week bootcamp!
